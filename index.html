Peer Observation Feedback Form

SPPS Peer Observation Program

Download Word template

1. Positive Aspects

Consider (from the perspective of the learner): Did the session meet its objectives? Were the teaching methods effective? Was the content clear and engaging throughout?

2. Areas for Improvement

Consider: Were there parts of the activity that were not engaging? Is there sufficient/inappropriate cross-over of UQ Extend materials?

3. Opportunities for Change

Consider: Is there something you would have done differently? Are there opportunities to experiment with novel methods of teaching? Are there opportunities to link UQ Extend materials more explicitly to workshop materials? Can you share some best practice tips?

4. Key Takeaways

What have you (the observer) seen that you would like to try in your class?

5. Additional Comments

Please share any additional thoughts or feedback about the session.
